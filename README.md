# Flow

Flow is a private, all-in-one productivity app that lives entirely in your
browser. No account, no sign-up, no ads — just you and your stuff.

## What you can do

**Tasks** — Keep track of everything. Add tasks in plain English: try
"Meeting tomorrow 2pm" or "Read chapter 5 every week". Set priorities, due
dates, subtasks, and track progress. Tasks you finish regularly repeat
automatically.

**Categories** — Group your tasks into Work, Personal, Learning, and more.
Create your own categories with custom colors. Filter by Today, Upcoming,
Favorites, or Pinned.

**Calendar** — See your month or week at a glance. Each day shows how many
tasks are due so you can plan ahead.

**Notes** — Write notes with a built-in editor and link them to tasks so
everything connected stays together.

**Tables** — Create spreadsheets for project plans, budgets, inventories,
or anything else. Format cells with bold, italic, colors, and alignment.
Resize columns, undo/redo, and import or export CSV files.

**Dashboard** — View your productivity trends, completion rates, category
breakdown, focus time stats, and your current streak — all in one place.

**Focus Timer** — Stay focused with a Pomodoro timer. Customize work and
break lengths, add sticky notes, and review your session history.

**Vault** — Store passwords securely. Copy them when needed. Everything
stays on your device.

**Quick Add** — Type tasks naturally and Flow picks up the date, priority,
and repeat schedule from your words.

**Search & Shortcuts** — Press Ctrl/Cmd+K to open the command palette,
`/` to search, and use keyboard shortcuts for nearly everything.

**Multi-Select** — Select several tasks at once to complete, delete, or
update them all together.

**Themes** — Switch between light, dark, or auto mode. Pick from 7 accent
colors, 3 text sizes, and compact or comfortable spacing.

**Privacy** — Your data stays in your browser. You can export a backup
anytime. No servers, no tracking, no account.

**Offline** — Once loaded, Flow works without internet. You can install it
on your phone or desktop home screen like a real app.
